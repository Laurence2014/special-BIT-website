WELCOME TO BEIJING INSTITUTE TECHNOLOGY


Study in BIT

BEIJING INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY(BIT)is a public university established in 1940 which has three campuses in Beijing, the Zhongguancun campus, the Liangxiang campus and the xishan experimentary area. It is mainly a school of technology but you can also find some other fields. It has english taught programms and chinese taught programms.

Zhongguancun campus

Beijing BIT's main campus is located in Zhuongguancun area of northwest of Beijing,Zhongguangcun campus has an area of 920,700 square meters, including a floor space of 724,000 square meters. It is the oldest and the used to be the only campus of BIT. Since the opening of Liangxiang campus in 2007, Zhongguancun campus is mainly used to host higher year undergraduate students and postgraduate students.

Liangxiang

Since 2007, lower year undergraduate students are hosted in Liangxiang campus, which is based in Liangxiang University Town, Fangshan District, southwest suburb of Beijing.

BIT has four programs

  • Undergraduate program
  • Graduate program
  • Postgraduate program
  • Language program

Library

Established in 1940, the BIT library now consists of one main library and four branch libraries. Nine new branch libraries are in construction or planning.

International student center

The International Office was established in 1999, is in charge of all international related affairs for BIT.

Student life

Student housing

All undergraduate and postgraduate students at BIT are guaranteed university accommodation. Allocation is normally on the basis of students' major, year and class etc. Both campuses also provide staff accommodation.
